Recipe for Shahi Panner

Description of the Dish

Shahi Paneer translates to "Royal Paneer” and has origins in Mughlai cuisine. It is a rich Indian curry prepared with luxury ingredients such as paneer cubes with a gravy of cream, nuts, whole spices, seeds, dried fruit and saffron.

In homes, the most popular paneer dishes are prepared during special occasions, parties and sometimes Diwali. These dishes often have a long list of ingredients but are guaranteed to tickle the taste buds of every single guest!

Ingredients for the Recipe:

For Onion Tomato Puree:

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 pods cardamom
  • 1 inch cinnamon
  • 1 pod black cardamom
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 onion , chopped>
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 inch ginger , chopped>
  • 2 tomatoes , chopped
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp salt

For the Curry:

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ½ tsp Shahi jeera (jeera)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri red chilli powder
  • ¼ cup cream/malai
  • 15 cubes paneer / cottage cheese
  • a few strands of saffron
  • ½ tsp kasuri methi (fenugreek leaves) , crushed
  • ¼ tsp garam masala

Steps and Instructions:

  1. First of all, heat 1 tbsp butter in a pan and fry 2 pods of cardamom, 1 inch cinnamon, 1 pod black cardamom and 3 cloves.
  2. Now add 1 onion, 3 cloves garlic and 1 inch ginger.
  3. Fry the onions a little until they become soft.
  4. After this, add 2 tomatoes and fry them a little.
  5. Apart from this, add 1 cup water and 1 teaspoon salt to it. Mix well.
  6. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until it becomes completely soft.
  7. Cool completely and transfer it to a blender. Blend to a smooth paste without adding any water.
  8. Strain the puree and make sure the puree is smooth and silky. Keep it aside.
  9. Heat 1 tablespoon butter in a large pan. Fry ½ teaspoon shahi jeera and 1 bay leaf.
  10. Keep it on low flame and add ¼ tsp turmeric and 1 tsp chilli powder.
  11. Saute for a while until the spices become aromatic.
  12. Add the prepared tomato-onion puree and mix well.
  13. next, add ¼ cup cream and mix until everything is well combined.
  14. Add 15 cubes paneer, few strands of saffron and mix well.
  15. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes or until the flavours are absorbed well.
  16. Now add ½ tsp kasuri methi and ¼ tsp garam masala. Mix well.
  17. Finally enjoy Shahi Paneer with roti or Butter Naan.
